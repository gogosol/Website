# Design Direction

**Aesthetic**: Enterprise cinematic, systems intelligence, analytical clarity. Calm confidence. Precision over hype. Minimalist and refined.

**Colors**: 
- **Background**: Deep navy / near-black (`#020617`, `#0F172A`).
- **Surfaces**: Dark slate / cool gray (`#1E293B`, `#334155`).
- **Text**: White for primary headers, soft gray for secondary copy. No pure harsh `#FFF` without intent.
- **Accents**: Restrained electric blue (`#0EA5E9`) or quantum violet (`#8B5CF6`). Only for interactive states or critical subtle data points.

**Typography**: 
- **Primary Font**: `Inter` (Neo-grotesk).
- **Scale**: Strong hero display sizes, very legible and crisp body copy, compact metadata labels. High contrast and sharpness.

**Layout**: 
- 12-column grid.
- Dense info blocks but spaced comfortably with ample padding ("structured density").
- Borders: Subtle (`border-slate-800`), layered overlapping panels to emulate deep technical interfaces.

**Motion**: 
- Framework: Framer Motion.
- Behavior: Very minimal. Fade ins on scroll, gentle upward Y-axis translations (20px to 0).
- Micro-interactions: Slight card hover background brightens or border highlights. No bouncing. 

**Visual Elements**: 
- No cartoon imagery.
- Vector iconography (Lucide React) with a minimal 1.5px stroke width.
- Emulate structured dashboards, evidence pipelines, data tables, and schematics to illustrate abstract platform concepts.
